  #title
  1\. Who are we?

  #description
  The nonprofit organisation Frigear, CVR-nr. 44353261
  :::

  :::u-page-card
  ---
  icon: i-lucide-file-user
  ---
  #title
  2\. What data do we collect?

  #description
  We collect the following information:

  - Name
  - Address
  - Email
  - Phone number
  - Payment information (when paying membership fees or purchasing goods, tickets, etc.)
  :::

  :::u-page-card
  ---
  icon: i-lucide-shield-question
  ---
  #title
  3\. How do we use your data?

  #description
  We use your information for the following purposes:

  - For membership administration
  - For newsletters (if you have given consent)
  - For planning and invitations to events
  - For collecting membership fees
  - For compliance with legal obligations
  - For use of third-party service providers (e.g. payment gateways)
  - For cooperation with partners on events and projects
  :::

  :::u-page-card
  ---
  icon: i-lucide-share-2
  ---
  #title
  4\. Sharing your information

  #description
  We do not share your information with third parties without your consent, unless required by law or necessary for bookkeeping, or external collaboration.
  :::

  :::u-page-card
  ---
  icon: i-lucide-database
  ---
  #title
  5\. Storage and deletion of data

  #description
  We store your information for as long as it is necessary for the above purposes.
  When you are no longer a member, your information is deleted after 12 months, unless we are required to keep it longer.
  :::

  :::u-page-card
  ---
  icon: i-lucide-scale
  ---
  #title
  6\. Your rights

  #description
  You have the right to:

  - Request access to your data
  - Have your information corrected or deleted
  - Restrict the processing of your data
  - Withdraw your consent
  - Complain to the Danish Data Protection Agency

  9\. Legal basis

  #description
  The processing of your personal data takes place on the basis of:
